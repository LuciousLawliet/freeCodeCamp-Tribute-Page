Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie

The Mr. Crack

The timeline of Mr. Habibie's life

1936 - Born in Afdeeling Parepare, Celebes, Hindia Belanda

1960–1965 - Research Assistant, Chair and Institute for Lightweight Construction, Rheinisch-Westfaelische Technische Hochschule (RWTH), Aachen, Germany - now RWTH Aachen University;

1969–1973 - Head, Division of Methods and Technology for Commercial and Military Transport Aircraft, MBB GmbH, Hamburg, Germany

1974–1978 - VP, Directorate of Technology Application, MBB GmbH, Hamburg, Germany

1976-1998 - President Director of PT Industri Pesawat Terbang Nurtanio (Persero) - established on April 26, 1976, as a merger of the Aviation Technology Division of ATTP PN Pertamina with the Nurtanio Aviation Industry Institute (LIPNUR) - now PT Dirgantara Indonesia (Persero)

1978-1998 - President Director of Perum Dock and Shipyard - established on March 29, 1978, from the Naval Training Command (KONATAL), previously known as the Naval Training (PAL) before 1970 - now PT PAL Indonesia (Persero)

1978-1998 - Chairman of the Batam Island Industrial Development Authority (Otorita Batam) - now the Batam Free Trade Zone and Free Port Authority (BP Batam)

1989-1998 - Chairman of the Strategic Industry Management Agency (BPIS) and Vice Chairman of the Strategic Industry Advisory Board (DPIS)

1998 - Vice President of the Republic of Indonesia

1998-1999 - President of the Republic of Indonesia

2019 - Dies at the age of 83.
